ABOUT THE ILLUSTRATOR

Since its humble beginnings in 2016, Outcasted Art has been my little corner of the world, a place where creativity meets spray paint. What started as a hobby, dabbling in spray paint art, gradually grew into so much more. Over the years, my love for the craft has spilled over into custom guitars, carefully drawn portraits, vibrant murals, and most recently, playful book illustrations. After seven years of dedication, I'm thrilled to share that I've built this modest art venture entirely on my own. The journey hasn't always been easy, but every brush stroke and spray has been worth it. Outcasted Art was never just about me. It was born from a desire to create, to give people a voice through art, and to find my own voice too. Now, after crafting countless original pieces for folks across the globe, I'm excited to see Outcasted Art growing in its own way. To every client who has trusted me to bring their vision to life, thank you. Your faith in my work has not only helped Outcasted Art grow, but it's also brought comfort and stability to my family of four. We're living a simple, debt-free life, and for that, I'm immensely grateful. As a shy kid, and a bit of an outcast myself, taking on a leadership role wasn't something I ever imagined. Outcasted Art was a dream, and despite my doubts, I took the leap. Today, it's my hope that every piece I create tells a story, my story, and the story of every outcast who dared to dream.
